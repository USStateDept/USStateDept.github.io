GitHub For The Department of State

To better comply with The White House's Open Government Initiative and President Obama's Executive Order -- Making Open and Machine Readable the New Default for Government Information, The Department of State requires a versatile, readily-available, and understandable outlet for the dispersal of open government data and code. GitHub has been a popular space for hosting versioned data in the public sector for some time. Recently, it has seen a good deal of participation from federal agencies such as the General Services Administration and Environmental Protection Agency.

The Office of eDiplomacy has established a presence for the Department of State on GitHub in the U.S. Department of State GitHub Organization.
